What is CLUB STAR STUDENT
CLUB STAR STUDENT is a gateway to connect students to all the clubs and activities going on around school, filling the void of conenctivity between students and clubs.
 How do I display my club?
In order to display your club you will need to navigate to the forms tab to submit a club, please keep in mind that the clubs tab is updated biweekly so your club may take up to 14 days to display.
